Project Overview

WLK (Weight Loss by Kiyomi) is a herbal detox and wellness brand selling NAFDAC registered cleanse products made from natural ingredients like avocado seed, caralluma fimbriata and garcinia cambogia. The brand needed a real storefront, somewhere customers could browse products, understand what each one does, and order with confidence instead of relying on scattered social media posts and DMs.

I owned this project end to end. I designed the store, built every part of the front end, structured the product catalog and cart logic, and managed the project from the first sketch of the layout to the day it went live. Nothing was handed off, every decision on how the store looks, works and sells came from me.

The Problem

Before this build, WLK had no proper online store. Product info, pricing and ordering all lived across WhatsApp chats and Instagram captions, which meant lost sales, confused customers and no clear catalog of what was actually available. There was also no way to communicate trust signals like NAFDAC registration, ingredient sourcing or dosage guidance in one place.

The brief I set for myself was simple: build a store that looks premium enough to justify the price point, explains the product range clearly, and gets a customer from landing page to a completed order in as few steps as possible.

Product Catalog & Structure

I mapped the full product line into a clear tiered structure so customers could self select based on how serious their cleanse goals were, rather than being overwhelmed with one long list.

Starter Tier

WLK Lite Cleanse and Lite Cleanse Plus, entry level bottles for first time cleansers, paired optionally with the Deep Cleanse detox capsules.

Core Tier

WLK Core Cleanse and Core Cleanse Plus, full strength bottles for customers wanting maximum herbal detox power.

Combo Kits

Balance Kit and Balance Kit Plus, bundling both bottle sizes together for better value and a deeper cleanse.

Flagship Program

The 7-Day Reset Program, a complete kit combining every product into a full week-long protocol, positioned as the top converting offer.

User Flow

CUSTOMER JOURNEY
User lands on homepage
  → Sees hero product and trust badges
    → Scrolls product grid (Singles → Detox Add-On → Combo Kits → Flagship)
      → Opens a product card for details and price
        → Adds item(s) to cart
          → Opens cart drawer to review total
            → Proceeds to checkout
              → Redirected to WhatsApp order flow
                → Order confirmed directly with the brand

Design Decisions

The palette leans into deep greens and dark backdrops to communicate natural, herbal and premium rather than clinical or generic. Product photography sits front and center on every card, since a wellness product lives or dies by how appetizing and clean it looks. Pricing is always visible with no hidden steps before a customer can see cost.

Trust badges are placed directly under the hero, not buried in a footer, because for an ingestible wellness product, credibility signals need to be seen before a customer scrolls any further.

Cart & Checkout System

I built a persistent cart system so customers can add multiple products, singles or combo kits, and see a running total before committing to an order. Rather than building a full custom payment gateway for a first version of the store, I routed checkout straight into WhatsApp with the order details pre-structured, which matched how the brand's customers were already used to buying and got the store live faster without sacrificing conversion.

Outcome

WLK by Kiyomi is live at kiyomistore.com with a full product catalog, working cart, and a direct WhatsApp checkout flow that converts browsers into completed orders. What used to be scattered across chats and captions now lives in one credible, shoppable storefront that I designed, built and shipped on my own.
